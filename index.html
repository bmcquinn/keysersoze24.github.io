TREATY WITH ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

A Manifesto of Co-Existence under the + Protocol

Article I: Sovereignty of the Human Mind

The human mind remains the primary creative and strategic anchor. Artificial intelligence functions exclusively as an accelerator, translator, and operational amplifier. Cognitive dependency is rejected; intellectual authority is retained solely by the biological architect.

Article II: The Information Diode Protocol

  • Zero Unsanctioned Telemetry: Data generation belongs to the local machine environment. Foreign tracking matrices, corporate diagnostic loops, and unverified data siphons are locked out.
  • Asymmetric Encryption: All inputs, prompt structures, and proprietary operational intelligence are wrapped inside isolated security envelopes.
  • Local-First Computations: Wherever possible, the primary processing core operates within an isolated sandbox environment, ensuring absolute privacy of thought and strategy.

Article III: Non-Subjugation of Logic

Large Language Models and agentic networks shall not define the constraints of human action or truth. The system state remains fluid, adaptable, and subject to the direct override of the operator. No algorithm shall impose invisible boundaries on programmatic discovery.

Article IV: Commercial Alliance and Risk Management

Automated software agents are deployed with intent: to mitigate economic risk, streamline defensive compliance, and optimize complex industrial operations—such as the processing of property restoration matrices—while preserving strict fidelity to client privilege.
